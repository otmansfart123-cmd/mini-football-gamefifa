⚽ Football World ⚽

Football is the most popular sport in the world. Millions of people watch and play it every day. It is a game of teamwork, strategy, and passion. Famous players like Messi and Ronaldo have inspired many young people to follow their dreams. Football is played between two teams of eleven players. The goal is to score by putting the ball into the opponent’s net. It requires speed, skill, and intelligence.

A goalkeeper is the last line of defense and must use reflexes, anticipation, and strength to stop the ball. Strikers, midfielders, and defenders all have different roles, making football a sport full of excitement and tactics. Every goal, save, and pass creates unforgettable moments for fans around the world.
